## 1. Overview
The Autism Health Tracker is designed to help you manage healthcare information, appointments, as well as medications related to autism health.

## 2. Using the Excel Templates

### 2.1 Doctor Information
- **Purpose:** This sheet allows you to store essential details about your healthcare provider(s).
- **Key Columns:**
  - Name
  - Specialty
  - Phone Number
  - Email Address
  - Notes (Any additional information you'd like to keep in mind)

### 2.2 Appointments
- **Purpose:** Record upcoming appointments and manage schedules.
- **Key Columns:**
  - Date & Time
  - Doctor Name
  - Reason for Visit
  - Location
  - Notes (What to discuss during the appointment)

### 2.3 Medications
- **Purpose:** Keep track of medications prescribed.
- **Key Columns:**
  - Medication Name
  - Dosage
  - Frequency
  - Doctor Prescribing
  - Start Date
  - Notes (Any side effects or observations)

### 2.4 Appointment History
- **Purpose:** Maintain a history of previous appointments for easy reference.
- **Key Columns:**
  - Date & Time
  - Doctor Name
  - Reason for Visit
  - Outcome
  - Follow-up Actions

## 3. Tips for Success
- **Regular Updates:** Ensure you regularly update your templates after each appointment or medication change.
- **Double Check Information:** Accuracy is important; always verify the information entered in the sheets.
- **Backup Your Files:** Keep a backup of your Excel files in case of accidental loss.
